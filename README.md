# Build and Battle

This is an HTML5 card and dice game playable in the browser.

## Files
- index.html
- style.css
- script.js

## Game Rules

Build and Battle is a two-player card and dice game. Player 2 is controlled by the computer.

### Phase 1: Card Collection
- Players take turns rolling two dice to determine who selects a card first.
- The selector rolls the dice; the opponent rolls to challenge.
- The higher roll wins the card. Ties result in rerolls.
- Cards are numbered 1-12.
- The first player to collect 6 cards wins all remaining cards.
- Both players end with 6 cards each.

### Phase 2: Head-to-Head Battle
- 6 rounds of play.
- Each round, both players select a card from their hand to play face down.
- Cards are revealed, and the higher card wins the round (1 point).
- Ties award no points.
- The player with more points after 6 rounds wins the game.
